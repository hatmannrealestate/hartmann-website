Firm


Hartmann Real Estate is a boutique European real estate development and investment firm dedicated to high‑quality residential and mixed‑use projects in carefully selected markets across Europe. We focus on well‑located but under-utilized assets, transforming them into architecturally refined properties where value is created through a clear strategic investment rationale, focused execution and clearly defined exit strategies for our equity partners.

We focus exclusively on real estate and commit our own capital alongside partners in projects where we can directly shape the outcome through design, capex and execution. Our work is grounded in operational fluency and first‑hand experience of how value is generated through the development of real estate, from concept and delivery to asset performance over time.

Every project is treated as both a product and a value‑creation plan: we design buildings for clearly identified demand and credible exit routes, aligning user needs, micro‑location and a robust investment thesis so that architectural quality, liveability and risk‑adjusted performance are built in from the start. Hartmann Real Estate operates as a full value‑chain platform with a track record across planning, development, investment and commercial transactions.

Background


Hartmann Real Estate was founded by Bruno Lara to combine a deep commitment to the built environment with a developer's execution experience and an institutional investment perspective. He has 15+ years of experience across the real estate value chain in the US and Europe, from planning and development to investment and commercial transactions, focused on residential and mixed‑use assets.

Bruno began his career in commercial architecture in Florida before moving into high‑end residential development in California, executing bespoke single‑ and multi‑family projects in locations such as Santa Monica, Pacific Palisades and Malibu. Running projects from concept through completion, he built an execution‑level understanding of how product specification, build quality and end‑user requirements translate into pricing power and risk control in luxury‑grade residential assets.

He later moved into value‑add residential investment and redevelopment, overseeing acquisitions, capex and execution for private investors, family offices and high‑net‑worth individuals, upgrading underperforming assets into higher‑yielding properties with defined exit strategies. The next step was joining BNP Paribas Real Estate in Germany, where he was promoted to Director International Investment after one year and spent over five years leading international mandates, sourcing off‑market opportunities and executing transactions across all major asset classes and risk profiles for inbound cross‑border institutional capital.

His academic background includes a Bachelor of Architecture from Florida Atlantic University, a Master in Real Estate Development from IE Business School, and current MBA candidacy at London Business School in London and Dubai. This combination of architectural, development and business training complements his professional experience and informs Hartmann Real Estate's analytical, value‑oriented approach to conceiving and delivering bespoke development projects for private and institutional equity partners, with a focus on generating attractive returns while mitigating risk.

Approach


We focus on opportunities where thoughtful design, active asset management and intelligent capital structuring can unlock value that is not yet reflected in pricing. That can mean repositioning or intensifying existing assets, solving planning or technical constraints, or bringing institutional discipline to smaller, more complex projects that fall below the radar of larger investors.

Across all projects, we aim to create assets that stand up to scrutiny in terms of design, function and performance. We focus on careful planning and well‑designed layouts, light, privacy and everyday usability, recognizing that it is the small architectural and specification decisions that ultimately convey quality. This is paired with robust, realistic capex planning and conservative assumptions on leasing, sales and exits, so that each project is grounded in both design intelligence and sound underwriting.

We also place strong emphasis on process and counterparties: working with best‑in‑class local partners, consultants and operators; engaging constructively with municipalities and stakeholders; and communicating transparently with equity partners throughout the life of a project. The objective is to align interests, manage risk early and create the conditions for successful execution and flexible exit options across different investment horizons.
